Rains Down Africa

Endless savanna plains, spectacular wildlife, high diversity of culture and an inspiring spice island… meet our home, Tanzania.

The Rains Down Africa (Rains Downers’) is here to help you plan and guide you on your dream African adventure. [Explore the Kilimanjaro snowy mountain](/mount-kilimanjaro) in the middle of hot Africa, the earliest evidence of the existence of human ancestors in Olduvai George, beautiful beaches, geological wonders, historical towns and archaeological sites. Tanzania is one of the last great places left on earth.

We offer personalised safaris, 100% tailored to your every need. Perfect for honeymooners, solo travellers or adventures with your family or friends.
